  • Tender Lettuce Salad

    When feeding a crowd, a large simple sala like this one is always a good idea.


    Prep: 10 mins
    Total: 10 mins
    Servings: 8

    Ingredients:

    • 2 tablespoons white-wine vinegar
    • 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
    • 1/2 teaspoon Dijon mustard
    • Coarse salt and ground pepper
    • 2 heads Boston lettuce (1 1/2 pounds total),
      torn into bite-size pieces
    • 2 cups mache or fresh parsley leaves

    Directions:

    In a small bowl, whisk together vinegar:

    1. olive oil, and mustard
    2. Season with salt and pepper
    3. In a large bowl, toss dressing with lettuce and mache.
    4. Serve immediately
